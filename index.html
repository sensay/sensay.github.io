ShiftData

It seems every day, another startup or ICO is launched which aims to give users access and control of their data. This is usually done by means of a token, a dapp or an application. However, this means that we are building de-centralized versions of these items. How many times have you entered your resume on job websites over the years. 10? 20 ? More ?

We have to stop the creation of new silos of data, which are not shared between interested actors. However, we do still want people to use and shift their data in civic over to selfkey, or datum. Ideally, this should be done using the websites or apps own erc-20 tokens. So what todo ?
